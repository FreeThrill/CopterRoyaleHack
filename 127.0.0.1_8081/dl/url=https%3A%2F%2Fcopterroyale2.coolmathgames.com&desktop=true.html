UPDATE 9-2-2022
For the first time in Copter Royale history we are adding 3 new Superpowers and a new Passive Power in one awesome update!
 
FIRE SHOTS: Destroy bases in blazing glory
MAGNET: Get enemy shots off your trail
CHARGE SHOT: Charge up a massive blast
LOOT DRONE: Automatically collects powerups
 
Each of these new powers was submitted through our Superpower Design Contest last year. Thank you to everyone who submitted their idea!

We hope you enjoy all the new content. Have fun out there, pilots!
